House of artisan cupcakes

The Classic Cupcake Company has been in service of gourmet cupcakes and confectioneries since 2010. We are striving to expand our range of products from cupcakes to birthday cakes, cake pops and many other delicious treats for every needs. The mission of our team isn’t just to provide you with heavenly cupcakes that tantalize the senses, but to also create an experience. Warm-hearted people, great atmosphere, and all the baked goods will whisk you back to a place, flooded with memories, of waking up on Christmas with the smell of baking in the air, or your mom’s ‘secret recipe.’
